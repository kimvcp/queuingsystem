Bank queuing system

As a customer
I want to get a notification from bank staff when my queue is next 
so that I can get myself ready for my business.

As a bank staff
I want to see the total waiting customer in my queue 
so that I can estimate the total time of working. 

As a bank staff
I want to have a time count for each customer's business
so that I can skip the customer who isn't queuing.
